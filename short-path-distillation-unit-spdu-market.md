<p><p>A Short Path Distillation Unit (SPDU) is a type of distillation equipment used in the separation of mixtures, specifically for compounds with high boiling points or those that are thermally sensitive. SPDU operates under reduced pressure, allowing for low-temperature distillations, thus minimizing the degradation of heat-sensitive materials. The process involves heating the mixture and passing the vapor through a short path, leading to efficient separation.</p><p>The global Short Path Distillation Unit (SPDU) market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers of this growth is the increasing demand for high-quality and pure distillates in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, food and beverages, and essential oils. SPDU offers superior separation capabilities, eliminating impurities and producing highly purified substances.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising adoption of SPDU in the cannabis industry is also contributing to the market's growth.
