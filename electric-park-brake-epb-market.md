<p><p>Electric Park Brake (EPB) is a system in modern vehicles that replaces the traditional handbrake with an electronic mechanism for enhanced safety and convenience. The market for EPB is segmented into two types: Cable Puller Type and Caliper Integrated Type. Cable Puller Type EPB uses electric motors to pull on the existing handbrake cable, while Caliper Integrated Type EPB integrates the electronic mechanism directly into the brake caliper. Both types provide efficient parking capabilities and offer benefits such as automatic release and hill-hold assistance, catering to different vehicle models and customer preferences.</p></p>
