<p><p>Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) Interlayers are used in the manufacturing of laminated glass, which is extensively used in the construction and automotive industries. There are two types of PVB interlayers: Standard Polyvinyl Butyral and Structural Polyvinyl Butyral. Standard PVB interlayers are commonly used in applications where safety and security are paramount, as they provide high impact resistance and prevent shattering. On the other hand, Structural PVB interlayers offer enhanced structural integrity and stiffness, making them suitable for architectural applications that require greater strength and durability.</p></p>
