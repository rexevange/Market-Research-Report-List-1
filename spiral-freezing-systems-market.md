<p><p>Spiral freezing systems are used in the food industry to freeze products quickly and efficiently. There are two main types of spiral freezers: single drum spiral freezers and double drum spiral freezers. Single drum spiral freezers have a single rotating drum where the products are placed for freezing, while double drum spiral freezers have two parallel rotating drums. Both types provide continuous freezing by transporting the products through a spiral conveyor belt, ensuring even and consistent freezing. These systems are widely used in various food processing applications to preserve product quality and extend shelf life.</p></p>
