<p><p>Organic Full-Fat Soymeal is a type of organic soymeal that contains the natural fats present in soybeans. It is made by grinding whole soybeans and retaining all of their oil content. This type of soymeal is rich in essential fatty acids and provides a higher energy content. On the other hand, Organic Defatted Soymeal is produced by extracting the oil from soybeans, resulting in a lower fat content. It has a higher protein concentration and is commonly used as a high-protein feed ingredient in livestock and poultry diets.</p></p>
