<p><p>The adhesive films market consists of various types, including pressure sensitive, hot-melt, light-cured, and others. Pressure sensitive adhesive films are characterized by their ability to form a bond when lightly pressed against a surface. Hot-melt adhesive films rely on heat to melt and create a bond. Light-cured adhesive films use ultraviolet light for curing and bonding. Other types of adhesive films include solvent-based, water-based, and reactive adhesive films, each with their unique characteristics and applications. These adhesive films are widely used in industries such as packaging, automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
