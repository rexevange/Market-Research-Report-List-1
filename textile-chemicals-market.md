Printing auxiliaries are used to enhance the printing process and provide desired colors and effects on textiles. Pretreatment auxiliaries are used to prepare the fabric for dyeing or printing, while finishing auxiliaries are used to improve the final appearance and properties of the fabric.</p></p>
