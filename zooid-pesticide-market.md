<p><p>The Zooid Pesticide market can be classified into four main types: Animal Toxin, Insect Hormone, Pheromone, and Others. Animal Toxin-based pesticides utilize toxins extracted from certain animals as a means to control pests. Insect Hormone-based pesticides disrupt the growth and development of insects, ultimately inhibiting their reproduction. Pheromone-based pesticides use substances that mimic the natural chemicals released by pests, disrupting their behavior and ability to mate. Lastly, the category of Others includes various approaches such as biological control agents, plant extracts, and microbial pesticides. These different types offer diverse strategies to effectively combat pests and protect crops.</p></p>
