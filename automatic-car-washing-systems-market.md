<p><strong>The Automatic Car Washing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gantry Car Washing Systems</li><li>Conveyor Tunnel Washing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic car washing systems can be categorized into two main types: gantry car washing systems and conveyor tunnel washing systems. Gantry car washing systems consist of a fixed structure with rollers or brushes that move back and forth over the vehicle, washing it from top to bottom. Conveyor tunnel washing systems, on the other hand, involve a conveyor system that moves the vehicle through a tunnel-like structure, where high-pressure water jets and brushes clean the car. Both systems offer efficient and convenient ways to wash cars automatically, catering to different customer preferences.</p></p>
