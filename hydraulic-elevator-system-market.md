<p><strong>The Hydraulic Elevator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holed Hydraulic</li><li>Holeless Hydraulic</li><li>Roped Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic elevator system market consists of three types: holed hydraulic, holeless hydraulic, and roped hydraulic. Holed hydraulic systems require a pit to be dug beneath the elevator shaft, accommodating the hydraulic cylinder. Holeless hydraulic systems do not require a pit, as the cylinder is mounted within the shaft and operates with a telescoping plunger. Roped hydraulic systems utilize both hydraulic power and ropes to facilitate the elevator's movement, offering a balanced and efficient mechanism that does not require a pit or a separate machine room.</p></p>
