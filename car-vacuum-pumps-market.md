<p><p>The car vacuum pumps market can be categorized into two types: electric vacuum pumps and mechanical vacuum pumps. Electric vacuum pumps operate using electricity and are commonly used in modern car models. They provide efficient and consistent vacuum pressure for various car systems, such as braking and emission control. On the other hand, mechanical vacuum pumps are driven by the engine's mechanical power and are found in older car models. They create vacuum pressure by using a series of valves and pistons to remove air from the car's systems.</p></p>
