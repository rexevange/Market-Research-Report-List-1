<p><p>The Automotive Grade Power Semiconductor Device Market includes various types of power semiconductor devices used in automotive applications. Diodes/Rectifiers are used for converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). Thyristors are used as switches in high-power applications. MOSFETs (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistors) control the flow of electric current. IGBTs (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors) combine the advantages of both MOSFETs and Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJTs) and are used in high-power applications. Finally, BJTs are used for amplification and switching applications in low-power circuits. These devices play a crucial role in powering various automotive electronic systems.</p></p>
