<p><p>Cold rolled electrical steel refers to a type of steel that is specifically processed to exhibit certain magnetic properties, suitable for use in electrical equipment. There are two main types of cold rolled electrical steel: oriented electrical steel and non-oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel has been carefully processed to have a preferred direction of magnetic properties, making it ideal for use in transformers and motors. On the other hand, non-oriented electrical steel does not have a specific preferred direction of magnetic properties, making it suitable for applications such as generators and magnetic shielding.</p></p>
