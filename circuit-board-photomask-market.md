<p><p>The circuit board photomask market consists of different types, including Quartz Mask, Soda Mask, Relief Plate, and Other masks. Quartz masks are made of high-purity quartz substrates, which provide excellent transparency and durability for precise circuit pattern replication. Soda masks, on the other hand, are made of soda-lime glass substrates and are generally more affordable. Relief plates are used for thick-film circuit boards, offering raised patterns for circuitry. The "Other" market segment includes alternative mask materials or technologies used in specific niche applications. Each type caters to unique requirements and preferences of circuit board manufacturing processes.</p></p>
