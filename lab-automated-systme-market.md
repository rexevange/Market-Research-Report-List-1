<p><p>The Lab Automated System market consists of three main types: Pre-analytical Automated Systems, Post-analytical Automated Systems, and Total Lab Automation Systems. Pre-analytical systems automate tasks before the actual analysis of samples, such as sample labeling and sorting. Post-analytical systems automate tasks after the analysis, like result interpretation and report generation. Total Lab Automation Systems, on the other hand, automate the entire laboratory workflow, from sample preparation to results delivery. These systems help improve efficiency, reduce human errors, and enhance overall laboratory productivity.</p></p>
