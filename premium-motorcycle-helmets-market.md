<p><p>Premium motorcycle helmets can be broadly classified into two types - full face helmets and open face helmets. Full face helmets provide complete protection to the rider's head, face, and chin. They feature a durable outer shell, a cushioned inner liner, and a sturdy chin guard that offers superior safety. Open face helmets, on the other hand, cover the head and ears but do not have a chin guard. They provide adequate protection but offer less coverage compared to full face helmets. The choice between the two types depends on the rider's preferences and the level of protection desired.</p></p>
