<p><p>Cerebral vascular stents are medical devices used to treat blockages in the blood vessels of the brain. These stents come in different types: tantalum, stainless steel, and nitinol. Tantalum stents are made of a biocompatible material with high visibility under imaging techniques. Stainless steel stents offer excellent radial strength and flexibility, ensuring proper blood flow through the vessel. Nitinol stents are known for their shape memory properties, allowing them to adapt to the vessel's shape once deployed. All three types are effective options for treating cerebral vascular blockages.</p></p>
