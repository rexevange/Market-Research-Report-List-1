<p><p>The office building ambient lighting market includes various types of lights such as recessed lights, surface-mounted lights, suspended lights, strip lights, and others. Suspended lights hang from the ceiling on cables or rods, offering a more decorative and artistic approach. Strip lights are long and narrow, often used for under-cabinet or cove lighting. The "others" category encompasses additional lighting options like track lights or wall sconces that can be employed based on specific needs or aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
