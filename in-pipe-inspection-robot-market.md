<p><p>An in-pipe inspection robot is a specially designed robotic device used for inspecting the condition of pipelines, specifically those located underground or within buildings. In-pipe inspection robots are crucial for preventative maintenance and ensuring the integrity and safety of pipeline systems.</p><p>The in-pipe inspection robot market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective inspection solutions.
