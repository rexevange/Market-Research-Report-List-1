<p><p>Biaxial Oriented (BO) Film refers to a type of plastic film which is processed using a biaxial orientation technique, resulting in enhanced mechanical and barrier properties. There are three main types of BO film: Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) film, Biaxially Oriented Polyester (BoPET) film, and Biaxially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) film. BOPP film is widely used in packaging applications due to its excellent clarity and high tensile strength. BoPET film is mainly used for electrical insulation and packaging purposes, known for its excellent heat resistance and dimensional stability. BOPA film is primarily used for flexible packaging applications due to its high puncture resistance and moisture barrier properties.</p></p>
