<p><p>E-Beam Crucibles are commonly used in electron beam evaporation processes for thin film deposition. The Round crucibles have a circular shape and offer even heat distribution, making them suitable for uniform deposition. Cone crucibles have a conical shape and provide focused beam spot, resulting in precise deposition control for small areas. Both crucible types are made from high-temperature materials that can withstand the intense heat generated by the electron beam. Their different shapes and designs cater to specific deposition requirements and applications.</p></p>
