<p><p>The Waste Fill-level Sensor market consists of two major types: Ultrasonic and Laser. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to measure the level of waste in containers. They emit sound waves that bounce off the waste and return, enabling the sensor to calculate fill levels accurately. On the other hand, Laser sensors use laser beams to determine waste levels. These sensors emit laser beams that reflect off the waste and are detected, allowing for precise fill-level measurements. Both types of sensors are important in waste management systems for efficient monitoring and disposal.</p></p>
