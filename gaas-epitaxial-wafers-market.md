<p><strong>The GaAs Epitaxial Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LEC Grown GaAs</li><li>VGF Grown GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaAs epitaxial wafers market comprises LEC Grown GaAs and VGF Grown GaAs. LEC Grown GaAs (Liquid Encapsulated Czochralski) involves growing GaAs crystals using a liquid encapsulant, which helps in reducing impurities. These wafers are known for their high purity and excellent crystalline quality. On the other hand, VGF Grown GaAs (Vertical Gradient Freeze) is a process where GaAs crystals are grown vertically, leading to uniform crystal quality. These wafers are valued for their high resistivity and low impurity levels, making them suitable for high-frequency devices.</p></p>
