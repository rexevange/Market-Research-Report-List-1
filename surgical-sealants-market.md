<p><p>Surgical sealants are used to close wounds and control bleeding during surgery. There are three main types of surgical sealants available in the market. Natural or biological sealants are derived from human or animal sources and promote clotting to stop bleeding. Synthetic sealants are made from chemical compounds and form a barrier to prevent leakage. Semi-synthetic sealants are a combination of natural and synthetic materials, providing both clotting and barrier properties. These different sealant types offer surgeons options to effectively manage and secure incisions during surgical procedures.</p></p>
