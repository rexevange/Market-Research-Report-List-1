<p><p>IC Test Sorting Machines are used in the semiconductor industry to categorize and sort integrated circuits based on their quality and performance. There are three main types of market segments for these machines: Pick-And-Place Handlers, Gravity-Feed Handlers, and Turret Handlers. Pick-And-Place Handlers utilize robotic arms to pick and place ICs onto testing sockets. Gravity-Feed Handlers rely on gravity to feed ICs onto the test sockets, which simplifies the handling process. Turret Handlers use a rotating turret to load and unload ICs onto the test sockets, ensuring high-speed testing capabilities.</p></p>
