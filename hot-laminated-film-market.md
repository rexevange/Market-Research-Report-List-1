<p><p>The hot laminated film market comprises several types, including BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) based film, BOPET (biaxially oriented polyester) based film, BOPA (biaxially oriented polyamide) based film, and others. BOPP based film is widely used due to its high tensile strength, low cost, and excellent clarity. BOPET based film offers superior barrier properties, high thermal stability, and good mechanical strength. BOPA based film exhibits exceptional puncture resistance, dimensional stability, and oxygen barrier. Other types in the market may include PVC (polyvinyl chloride) based film, which is food-safe, and CPP (cast polypropylene) based film known for its excellent heat sealability and transparency.</p></p>
