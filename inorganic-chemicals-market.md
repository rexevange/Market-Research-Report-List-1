Inorganic salts are compounds made up of positive and negative ions, such as sodium chloride (table salt). Metal oxides are compounds formed by the combination of metals and oxygen. Alkali refers to a group of water-soluble compounds that are bases, like sodium hydroxide. Inorganic acids are substances that release hydrogen ions in water, like hydrochloric acid. These different types of inorganic chemicals are widely used in various industries due to their diverse properties.</p></p>
