<p><strong>The HIV Self Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow Immunoassay</li><li>Immunofiltration</li><li>Fluorescent Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HIV Self Test Kits market offers three main types of testing methods: Lateral Flow Immunoassay, Immunofiltration, and Fluorescent Chromatography. Lateral Flow Immunoassay is a simple and rapid method that uses colored bands to indicate the presence of HIV antibodies. Immunofiltration is a more sensitive method that utilizes nitrocellulose membranes to capture and detect HIV antigens. Fluorescent Chromatography employs fluorescent dyes to identify HIV biomarkers, providing higher accuracy. These types of HIV self-test kits offer individuals a convenient and private way to test for HIV in the comfort of their own homes.</p></p>
