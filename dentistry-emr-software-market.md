<p><p>Dentistry EMR software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessible through the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, easy upgrades, and remote access to patient data. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed and hosted locally on the dentist's servers or computers. It provides complete control over data and security but requires maintenance and hardware setup. Dentists can choose between these two types based on their specific needs, preferences, and budget.</p></p>
