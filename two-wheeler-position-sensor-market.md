<p><p>The two-wheeler position sensor market is categorized into various types such as capacitive, magnetic, infrared (IR), force sensor, and others. Capacitive sensors utilize changes in electric field to detect the position of the two-wheeler. Magnetic sensors make use of magnetic fields to determine the position. Infrared sensors use infrared light beams to sense the position of the two-wheeler. Force sensors rely on pressure or mechanical stress to identify the position. Other types refer to sensors that utilize different technologies, like ultrasonic or optical, to track the position of the two-wheeler.</p></p>
