It is available in two market types - internal mounted and external mounted. Internal mounted BPS refers to systems installed inside the aircraft, usually in the fuselage or cargo compartment. On the other hand, external mounted BPS is attached to the exterior of the aircraft, typically on the roof or underside. These market types offer different solutions for aircraft manufacturers and operators based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
