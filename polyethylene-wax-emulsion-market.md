<p><p>The polyethylene wax emulsion market can be categorized into three types based on their properties. Firstly, cationic properties indicate that the emulsion carries a positive charge, making it suitable for applications such as coating, adhesives, and textiles. Secondly, anionic characteristics refer to an emulsion with a negative charge, making it suitable for applications like paper coatings, inks, and paints. Lastly, non-ionic properties signify the absence of any charge, making it versatile and suitable for a wide array of applications in industries like construction, rubber, and plastics.</p></p>
