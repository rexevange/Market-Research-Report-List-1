<p><p>The electric jet ski market consists of three main types: Battery Electric Jet Ski, Plug-In Hybrid Electric Jet Ski, and Hybrid Electric Jet Ski. Battery Electric Jet Skis run solely on an electric motor powered by a rechargeable battery, offering zero-emission operation, but limited range. Plug-In Hybrid Electric Jet Skis combine an electric motor and a small internal combustion engine, providing longer range by switching between electric and gas power. Hybrid Electric Jet Skis utilize both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine simultaneously, optimizing performance and range while minimizing emissions.</p></p>
