<p><p>The pipeline anti-corrosion wrapping adhesive tape market is categorized into three types: petrolatum-based, polymer-based, and others. </p><p>1. Petrolatum-based tapes are composed of a mixture of petrolatum, mineral fillers, and stabilizers. They provide excellent long-term corrosion protection and are commonly used for below-ground pipelines.</p><p>2. Polymer-based tapes are made from synthetic materials such as polyethylene or polypropylene. They offer high strength and durability, making them suitable for both below-ground and above-ground pipelines.</p><p>3. The "others" category includes tape types that do not fit into the above two classifications, such as bitumen-based tapes or rubber-based tapes. These tapes can provide unique properties depending on the specific composition.</p></p>
