<p><p>Plastic barrier laminated tubes are used in various industries for packaging purposes. The market for these tubes can be categorized into three types based on the barrier materials used. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) laminated tubes provide excellent oxygen and gas barrier properties, making them suitable for products like cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Polyamide (PA) laminated tubes have good barrier properties against moisture and light, making them ideal for food and beverage packaging. Polyester (PET) laminated tubes offer good chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for industrial packaging applications.</p></p>
