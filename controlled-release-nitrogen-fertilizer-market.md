<p><p>Controlled-release nitrogen fertilizer is available in different market types including nitrate form, ammonia form, ammonium form, and urea form. The nitrate form of controlled-release nitrogen fertilizer releases nitrogen slowly and is suitable for crops requiring continuous feeding. The ammonia form is beneficial for crops with high nitrogen requirements. Ammonium form releases nitrogen quickly and is suitable for crops needing a rapid nitrogen supply. The urea form is ideal for all crops and provides a steady release of nitrogen over time. Each market type offers specific benefits tailored to different crop needs.</p></p>
