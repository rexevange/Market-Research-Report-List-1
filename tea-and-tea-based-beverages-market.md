<p><p>Tea is a widely consumed beverage that is prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant. Supermarkets/hypermarkets are the most preferred channels for purchasing tea, although online platforms are witnessing significant growth due to the convenience they offer. Regionally, Asia-Pacific is the largest market for tea, with China and India being the major consumers and producers.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards premium and specialty teas. Unique flavors, organic and sustainable production methods, and innovative packaging are becoming important factors for consumers.
