Molybdenum is used in the production of vehicle parts, such as engine blocks, crankshafts, and camshafts, due to its high strength and temperature resistance. The automotive industry's shift towards lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles is driving the demand for molybdenum-based alloys.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, is creating opportunities for the molybdenum market.
