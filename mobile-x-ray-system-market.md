<p><p>The mobile X-ray system market consists of two main types: motorized mobile X-ray systems and manual-driven mobile X-ray systems. Motorized mobile X-ray systems are equipped with motorized components that allow for easy movement and positioning of the X-ray unit. They offer enhanced maneuverability and versatility in various healthcare settings. On the other hand, manual-driven mobile X-ray systems require physical effort for movement and positioning. While they may not have the same level of mobility as motorized systems, they are more cost-effective and suitable for limited-budget facilities.</p></p>
