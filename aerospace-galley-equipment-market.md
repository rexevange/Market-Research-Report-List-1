<p><p>The aerospace galley equipment market consists of three main types: galley inserts, galley carts, and galley frames. Galley inserts are removable modules used to store food, beverages, and other supplies in an aircraft galley. Galley carts are trolley-like structures that transport these inserts within the cabin. Galley frames, on the other hand, provide the structural support and framework for the galley area. These three market segments play crucial roles in ensuring efficient and organized catering services on an aircraft.</p></p>
