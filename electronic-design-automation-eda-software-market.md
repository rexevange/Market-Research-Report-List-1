<p><p>Electronic Design Automation (EDA) Software market consists of several types. System-in-Package (SIP) software focuses on integrating multiple electronic components into a single package. Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) software aids in the design, simulation, and analysis of electronic systems. Printed Circuit Board (PCB) software facilitates the design and layout of circuit boards. Multichip Module (MCM) software enables the assembly and integration of multiple chips onto a single module. These software types collectively contribute to improving the efficiency and productivity of the electronic design process.</p></p>
