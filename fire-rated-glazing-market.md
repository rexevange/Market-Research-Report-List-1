<p><p>The fire-rated glazing market consists of various types of glass designed to resist fire and prevent its spread. Laminated Fire Resistant Glass is made by sandwiching a layer of fire-resistant material between two layers of glass. Wired Fire Resistant Glass has embedded metal wires that hold the glass in place even when exposed to high temperatures. Tempered Fire Resistant Glass is strengthened through a heating and cooling process to make it more resistant to fire. Ceramic Fire Resistant Glass is made with a ceramic interlayer that provides excellent fire protection. Other types may include specially treated or coated glass to enhance fire resistance.</p></p>
