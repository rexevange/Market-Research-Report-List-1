<p><p>Powder waterproof coating refers to a type of coating that is applied to surfaces to protect them from water damage. The market for powder waterproof coatings can be categorized into two main types: liquid waterproof coatings and dry waterproof coatings. Liquid waterproof coatings are applied in liquid form and then dry to form a protective barrier against moisture. On the other hand, dry waterproof coatings are pre-mixed powders that are mixed with water or a solvent before application. Both types provide effective protection against water penetration and offer different advantages based on their application requirements.</p></p>
