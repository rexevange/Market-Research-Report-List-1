<p><p>The military surveillance drones market consists of three main types: fixed-wing drones, rotary blade drones, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones are similar to airplanes, using forward thrust to generate lift and travel long distances efficiently. Rotary blade drones, also known as quadcopters, use multiple blades that rotate to generate lift and achieve stability, enabling them to maneuver in tight spaces. Hybrid drones combine features of both fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, allowing them to take off and land vertically like quadcopters while also having the ability to fly long distances like fixed-wing drones.</p></p>
