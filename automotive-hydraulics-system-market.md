<p><p>The automotive hydraulics system market consists of various types, including brake, clutch, suspension, and tappet. The brake hydraulic system helps in stopping or slowing down the vehicle by transmitting force from the brake pedal to the brake pads. The clutch hydraulic system enables the smooth engagement and disengagement of the engine power to the transmission. The suspension hydraulic system ensures a comfortable and stable ride by absorbing shocks and vibrations. The tappet hydraulic system is utilized for the precise and smooth actuation of engine valves.</p></p>
