<p><p>Dental intraoral x-rays market includes various types of x-ray examinations used for diagnostic and treatment purposes. Bitewing x-rays are commonly employed to visualize the upper and lower teeth in one area of the mouth, helping detect cavities between teeth and bone loss. Periapical x-rays focus on individual teeth, providing detailed images of the entire tooth from the crown to the root, suitable for assessing dental infections and impacted teeth. Other types of intraoral x-rays encompass occlusal x-rays that capture a broad view of the floor of the mouth, aiding in locating foreign objects or abnormalities.</p></p>
