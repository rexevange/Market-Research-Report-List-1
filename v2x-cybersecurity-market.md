<p><p>V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) cybersecurity market consists of two major types: On-Board Units (OBU) and Roadside Units (RSU). OBU refers to the in-vehicle devices that enable communication with other vehicles and roadside infrastructure. These units require robust cybersecurity measures to protect from potential attacks and ensure safe data transfer. RSU, on the other hand, are placed alongside roadways and provide connectivity to vehicles passing by. They also demand cybersecurity solutions to safeguard against unauthorized access and keep the communication network secure. Overall, both OBU and RSU segments of V2X market necessitate reliable cybersecurity measures for efficient and secure vehicle-to-everything communication.</p></p>
