Gait analysis examines the way individuals walk and move to identify and verify their identity. Signature analysis analyzes distinctive features of an individual's signature for identification purposes. Voice recognition utilizes the unique vocal characteristics of individuals to authenticate their identity. These market types aim to provide secure and reliable authentication solutions based on behavioral traits.</p></p>
