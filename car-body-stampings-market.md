<p><p>The car body stampings market encompasses two major types: aluminum and carbon steel. They also provide better corrosion resistance compared to carbon steel stampings. On the other hand, carbon steel stampings are more cost-effective and commonly used in automotive manufacturing due to their excellent formability and impact resistance.
