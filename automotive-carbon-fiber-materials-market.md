<p><p>The automotive carbon fiber materials market is categorized into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastics. Thermosetting carbon fiber materials are rigid and durable, making them suitable for applications that require high strength and stiffness. They are used in various automotive components, such as body panels and chassis. On the other hand, thermoplastics carbon fiber materials can be reshaped multiple times under high temperature. They offer greater design flexibility and are used in applications like interior components and lightweight structural parts in the automotive industry.</p></p>
