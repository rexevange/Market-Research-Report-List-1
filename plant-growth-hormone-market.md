<p><p>Plant growth hormone market types refer to the different types of hormones that regulate plant growth and development. Auxins are responsible for cell elongation and root development. Cytokinins promote cell division, leaf expansion, and delay leaf senescence. Gibberellins control stem elongation, fruit development, and seed germination. These hormones play a crucial role in various agricultural and horticultural practices, as they can be used to enhance crop productivity, induce rooting, regulate flowering, and improve fruit quality. The market for these hormones is driven by the demand for improved agricultural productivity and sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
