<p><p>The Automotive Retainer Market consists of three main types: Push Type, Threaded Type, and Others. Push Type retainers are typically used to secure components together by applying pressure, ensuring a tight fit. Threaded Type retainers, on the other hand, provide a threaded connection, allowing for easier installation and removal. Lastly, the Others category includes various types of retainers that do not fall into the specific push or threaded classifications, such as snap-in or bolt-on retainers. These different types offer flexibility in securing automotive components, suiting different needs and preferences.</p></p>
