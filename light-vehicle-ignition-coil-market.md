<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The light vehicle ignition coil market consists of two main types: single-spark and multi-spark. In the single-spark market, the ignition coil generates a single spark per ignition cycle, leading to a smooth and efficient combustion process. On the other hand, the multi-spark market employs ignition coils that generate multiple sparks per ignition cycle, enhancing the fuel-burning efficiency and overall performance of the vehicle. This innovation allows for better power output, improved fuel economy, and reduced emissions. Both types aim to optimize the ignition process in light vehicles for enhanced performance and efficiency.</p></p>
