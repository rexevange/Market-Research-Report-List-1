<p><strong>The Waterproof Flooring Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Urethane Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof flooring adhesives are classified into three types based on their composition - water-based, solvent-based, and urethane-based. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low volatile organic compound (VOC) content. Solvent-based adhesives contain chemicals that can dissolve in solvents, making them more effective in challenging conditions. Urethane-based adhesives offer excellent strength and durability and are resistant to moisture and temperature fluctuations. These different types cater to various flooring requirements, providing options for different installation environments and ensuring long-lasting and reliable adhesion.</p></p>
