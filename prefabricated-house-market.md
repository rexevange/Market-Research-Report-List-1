<p><p>A prefabricated house, also known as a prefab house or a modular house, is a type of building that is manufactured in off-site factories in separate sections or modules. Prefabricated houses have gained popularity due to their cost-effectiveness, speed of construction, and flexibility in design.</p><p>The future outlook of the prefabricated house market is promising. The industry is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years, driven by various factors such as the increasing demand for affordable housing, the need for quicker construction methods, and the growing focus on sustainable and energy-efficient buildings. Additionally, advancements in technology and manufacturing processes are making prefabricated houses more efficient, attractive, and customizable.</p><p>The current outlook of the prefabricated house market is also positive. The market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, with many countries and regions actively embracing prefabrication as a viable solution to their housing needs. Governments in several countries are encouraging the use of prefabricated houses to address housing shortages and promote affordable housing. Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of efficient and rapid construction methods, further boosting the demand for prefabricated houses.</p><p>The projected growth rate of the prefabricated house market, with a CAGR of 5.4% during the forecasted period, indicates a favorable market landscape.
