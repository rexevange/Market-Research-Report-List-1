<p><p>Water-based coatings are a type of paint or coating that uses water as the primary solvent instead of traditional organic solvents. This makes them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. There are three main types of water-based coatings: water-soluble paint, water dilution coating, and water dispersion coatings. Water-soluble paint dissolves completely in water and is used for various applications. Water dilution coatings require mixing with water before use, providing better coverage and reducing VOC emissions. Water dispersion coatings use water as a dispersing medium for pigments and additives, resulting in a stable and durable coating.</p></p>
