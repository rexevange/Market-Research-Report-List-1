<p><p>SMD ECG Monitor market encompasses two types of products: Holter ECG Tester and Static ECG Tester. A Holter ECG Tester is a portable device that records and monitors a patient's heart activity continuously for 24 to 48 hours. It is commonly used for diagnosing irregular heart rhythms and identifying potential cardiac issues. On the other hand, a Static ECG Tester is a stationary device used in clinics or hospitals to measure and evaluate a patient's heart activity during a specific period. It provides a detailed analysis to aid in diagnosing heart conditions accurately.</p></p>
