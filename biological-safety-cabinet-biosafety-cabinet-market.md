<p><p>The Biological Safety Cabinet (Biosafety Cabinet) market consists of three main types: Class II Type A, Class II Type B, and Class III. Class II Type A cabinets provide personnel and product protection by recirculating 70% of the filtered cabinet air, while also exhausting 30% to the room. Class II Type B cabinets feature total exhaust, providing both personnel and environmental protection. Class III cabinets provide the highest containment level, operating with a completely enclosed system, and require users to work through gloves attached to the cabinet.</p></p>
