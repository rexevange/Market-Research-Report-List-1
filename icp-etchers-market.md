<p><strong>The ICP Etchers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Load</li><li>Lock-Load</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ICP etchers are used in semiconductor manufacturing to etch patterns on silicon wafers. There are two main market types for ICP etchers: open-load and lock-load. Open-load systems have a simple structure where the wafer is placed on a fixed electrode and exposed to plasma. However, in lock-load systems, the wafer is clamped between two electrodes, providing better stability and control during etching. This prevents any movement or misalignment of the wafer, ensuring precise and accurate pattern transfer. Both types have their advantages and are utilized based on the specific requirements of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
