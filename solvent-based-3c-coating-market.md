<p><p>Solvent-based 3C coating market encompasses two main types: thermosetting coating and UV curing coating. Thermosetting coating is a type of coating that undergoes a chemical reaction when exposed to heat, resulting in a hardened and durable finish. It is widely used in the 3C (computer, communication, and consumer electronics) industry due to its high resistance to chemicals and heat. On the other hand, UV curing coating is a process that utilizes ultraviolet light to instantly cure and dry the coating, offering a quick and efficient solution for the 3C industry's coating needs.</p></p>
