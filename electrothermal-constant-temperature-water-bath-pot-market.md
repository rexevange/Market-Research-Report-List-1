<p><p>The Electrothermal Constant Temperature Water Bath Pot is available in two types: the Single Hole Constant Temperature Water Bath Pot and the Porous Constant Temperature Water Bath Pot. The Single Hole Pot has only one opening to regulate temperature, making it suitable for maintaining a constant temperature in one sample. On the other hand, the Porous Pot has multiple openings, allowing it to hold multiple samples simultaneously at a constant temperature. Both types are used in scientific and research laboratories for various applications that require precise temperature control.</p></p>
