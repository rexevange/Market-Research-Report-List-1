<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Superconducting Magnet Power Supply Market consists of two main types: Monopolar and Bipolar. A Monopolar power supply operates with a single electrical polarity, creating a magnetic field by circulating current through a superconducting magnet. On the other hand, a Bipolar power supply operates with both positive and negative polarities, allowing for the creation of a variable magnetic field. This type is commonly used in scientific research and medical applications where versatile and adjustable magnetic fields are required for experiments or diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
