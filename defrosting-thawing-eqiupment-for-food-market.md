<p><p>Defrosting and thawing equipment are used in the food market to safely thaw and defrost frozen food products. There are two main types of equipment available: chamber type and rotary type. Chamber type equipment consists of a compartment where frozen food is placed for thawing, using a controlled temperature and humidity environment. Rotary type equipment, on the other hand, uses rotating shelves to evenly distribute airflow and heat for faster thawing. Both types are designed to ensure food safety and maintain product quality while thawing frozen items.</p></p>
