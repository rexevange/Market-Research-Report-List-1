<p><p>Construction glass sealants are used to provide a secure and durable seal between glass surfaces in buildings. There are two types of sealants available in the market. Single component sealants are ready-to-use and do not require any mixing. They are convenient and easy to apply, saving time and effort. These sealants are often used for more complex applications that require specific properties or performance levels.</p></p>
