<p><p>The airlaid nonwoven paper market consists of three main types: latex-bonded, thermal-bonded, and multi-bonded airlaid paper. Latex-bonded airlaid paper is produced by bonding fibers with latex emulsion, resulting in high absorption and softness, making it suitable for applications such as wipes and personal care products. Thermal-bonded airlaid paper is created by using heat and pressure to bond fibers, offering excellent strength and absorbency for industrial uses like filtration. Lastly, multi-bonded airlaid paper combines the advantages of both latex and thermal bonding techniques, making it versatile for various applications from hygiene products to packaging materials.</p></p>
