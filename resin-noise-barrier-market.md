<p><p>Resin noise barriers are designed to reduce noise pollution in various settings. Reflective type noise barriers are made of hard materials that reflect sound waves, redirecting them away from the source. Absorptive type noise barriers, on the other hand, are made of softer materials that absorb sound waves, effectively reducing noise levels. Mixed type noise barriers combine both reflective and absorptive materials for enhanced noise reduction. Each of these market types target different applications, such as highways, railways, airports, industrial areas, and residential areas, helping to minimize noise impact on surrounding environments.</p></p>
