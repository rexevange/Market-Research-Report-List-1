Smart meters are the primary component of AMI, allowing two-way communication between the utility and the customer's meter. The communication system facilitates data transmission between the meter and utility back-end systems. Meter Data Management Systems (MDMS) collect, validate, and process the data received from smart meters. Together, these market types form a comprehensive infrastructure that enables real-time monitoring, billing accuracy, and demand response programs for electric utilities.</p></p>
