The market for polyisocyanurate insulation can be categorized into two types: Spray type and Foam/Board type. </p><p>Spray type polyisocyanurate insulation is applied as a liquid and expands upon contact with air, forming a solid foam insulation layer. This type is commonly used for insulating small gaps and irregularly shaped areas.</p><p>Foam/Board type polyisocyanurate insulation is manufactured as rigid foam boards, offering excellent insulation properties. These boards are widely used for insulating walls, roofs, and floors in both residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
