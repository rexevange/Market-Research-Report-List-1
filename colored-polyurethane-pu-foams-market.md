<p><p>Colored polyurethane (PU) foams are available in two main types - rigid and flexible. Rigid polyurethane foams have a high density and are used primarily for insulation and structural purposes in industries like construction and automotive. They provide excellent thermal insulation and structural support. On the other hand, flexible polyurethane foams have a lower density and are mainly used for cushioning and comfort applications, such as furniture, bedding, and automotive seating. They offer superior comfort and resilience, making them ideal for applications where flexibility and comfort are essential.</p></p>
