<p><p>Cored wire is a product widely used in the steelmaking process to introduce various elements into the molten metal. There are different types of cored wires available in the market. Calcium Silicide Cored Wire (CaSi) contains calcium silicide, which helps in deoxidation of the steel and improves its conductivity. Calcium Solid Cored Wire (Ca) is used for desulfurization and deoxidation purposes. Pure Carbon Cored Wire (C) is used for carbon pickup in the steel. Ferro Calcium Cored Wire (CaFe) aids in deoxidation and desulfurization. Other types of cored wires cater to specific alloying requirements and customization needs of the industry.</p></p>
