<p><p>The bullet train market consists of two main types: wheel on rail and maglev. Wheel on rail technology, also known as conventional or high-speed rail, uses wheels on tracks to propel the train forward. It is widely implemented across the world and offers relatively lower-speed operations. On the other hand, maglev (magnetic levitation) technology utilizes magnetic forces to suspend and propel the train above the track, eliminating any contact between the train and the rail. Maglev trains can achieve higher speeds and smoother rides, although they are less common compared to wheel on rail systems.</p></p>
