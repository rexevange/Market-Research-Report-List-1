<p><p>Compressor fluids can be classified into two main types: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oil is a highly engineered lubricant that is formulated with advanced additives to provide superior performance, such as improved oxidation resistance and better thermal stability. It is often used in applications where high temperatures, extreme pressures, or harsh operating conditions are present. On the other hand, mineral oil is a basic lubricant derived from crude oil. It offers good lubrication properties but may not perform as well in demanding environments compared to synthetic oil.</p></p>
