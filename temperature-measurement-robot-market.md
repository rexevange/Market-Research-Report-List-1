<p><p>The Temperature Measurement Robot market comprises of two main types: Infrared Imaging and Thermal Imaging. Infrared Imaging technology uses special sensors to detect infrared radiation emitted by objects. It measures temperature by capturing the amount of radiation and converting it into temperature readings. On the other hand, Thermal Imaging uses advanced cameras to capture the heat patterns emitted by objects and converts them into thermal images. Both technologies are widely used for non-contact temperature measurement in various industries, such as manufacturing, healthcare, and building inspections.</p></p>
