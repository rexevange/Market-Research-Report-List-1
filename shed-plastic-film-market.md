<p><p>Shed plastic film, also known as greenhouse plastic film, is a type of protective covering used in agriculture and horticulture programs. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of greenhouse farming practices globally is driving the demand for shed plastic film. As farmers look for efficient ways to protect their crops from adverse weather conditions and pests, the use of shed plastic film becomes essential. </p><p>Additionally, the growing consumer demand for organic and locally grown produce also contributes to the market growth. Shed plastic film provides the necessary conditions for controlled cultivation, resulting in higher crop yields and quality produce.
