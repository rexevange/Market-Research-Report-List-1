<p><p>The High Speed AEB (Automatic Emergency Braking) System market includes three major types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts drivers of an imminent collision, enabling them to take necessary evasive actions. Dynamic Brake Support increases brake pressure when a driver fails to apply enough force during emergency braking. Crash Imminent Braking automatically applies brakes to reduce impact severity or avoid a collision when the driver fails to react in time. These technologies aim to enhance driver safety and prevent accidents on the road.</p></p>
