<p><ul><li>Wet Process Type</li><li>Dry Process Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Infant Formula Milk Powder market can be categorized into two types: Wet Process Type and Dry Process Type. The Wet Process Type involves the use of fresh organic milk as a base, which is then processed, pasteurized, and homogenized. This type of formula tends to have a higher content of nutrients and is more similar to natural breast milk. On the other hand, the Dry Process Type involves the evaporation of water from the fresh organic milk, resulting in a concentrated powder form that can be reconstituted with water when needed. Both types offer organic alternatives to traditional infant formula options.</p></p>
