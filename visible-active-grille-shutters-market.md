<p><p>Visible active grille shutters are a component of a vehicle's grille system that can open or close automatically to control airflow and optimize aerodynamic efficiency. They are typically made of a series of horizontal slats or flaps that can be adjusted based on the vehicle's speed, temperature, and cooling needs. When the shutters are closed, they reduce drag and improve fuel efficiency by redirecting airflow around the vehicle. On the other hand, when open, they enable airflow to cool the engine and other components.</p><p>The visible active grille shutters market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
