<p><p>Vibratory finishing equipment refers to machinery used for finishing and polishing surfaces of various materials. It is available in different types: centrifugal, linear, and CNC. Centrifugal vibratory finishing equipment utilizes rotational force to achieve the desired finishing effect. Linear vibratory finishing equipment uses linear motion to achieve the desired finish. CNC vibratory finishing equipment incorporates computer numerical control to enhance precision and automate the finishing process. Each type of equipment caters to different needs and preferences in terms of surface finishing.</p></p>
