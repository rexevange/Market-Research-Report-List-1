<p><p>Electronic Caries Monitors (ECM) are advanced dental tools used for the early detection and monitoring of tooth decay. ECMs help dentists identify caries at an early stage, enabling timely intervention and prevention of further damage.</p><p>The market for Electronic Caries Monitors is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include increasing awareness about dental health, the rising prevalence of dental caries and cavities, and the growing emphasis on preventive dentistry.
