<p><p>Natural stone and marble are materials used in construction that are derived from the earth's crust. Natural stone refers to any rock or stone that is naturally occurring and has been quarried for various purposes such as countertops, flooring, and paving stones. Marble, specifically, is a type of natural stone that is formed from limestone through a process of metamorphism where high temperatures and pressures cause the stone to recrystallize.</p><p>The natural stone and marble market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from the construction industry. Rising urbanization and infrastructure development, particularly in emerging economies, are contributing to the robust market growth. Engineered stone, also known as quartz, is a manufactured product that combines natural stone aggregates with resins and pigments. It offers enhanced durability, as well as a wider range of colors and patterns compared to natural stone. The growing demand for engineered stone is driven by its ability to mimic the appearance of natural stone while providing added benefits such as resistance to stains and scratches.</p><p>Another notable trend is the rising adoption of technology in the natural stone and marble industry. Technologies such as 3D printing and robotic fabrication are being utilized to enhance precision, improve efficiency, and reduce waste in the manufacturing and installation processes.</p><p>In conclusion, the natural stone and marble market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue thriving in the coming years.
