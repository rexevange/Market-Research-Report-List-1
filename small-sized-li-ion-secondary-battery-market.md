<p><p>The small-sized Li-ion secondary battery market comprises three main types: cylindrical cell, prismatic cell, and polymer cell. Cylindrical cells are the most widely used and offer a good balance between energy density and cost. Prismatic cells are flat and rectangular in shape, making them suitable for thin devices with limited space. Polymer cells, on the other hand, are flexible and can be easily molded into any shape, offering design flexibility. Each type has its advantages, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option based on the specific requirements of their products.</p></p>
