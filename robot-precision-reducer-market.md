<p><p>The Robot Precision Reducer market consists of two main types: RV Reducer and Harmonic Reducer. On the other hand, Harmonic Reducers are known for their high precision and accuracy. They use a flexible spline to achieve motion control, making them suitable for applications requiring positioning accuracy, such as robotic arms and CNC machines. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of robotic systems.</p></p>
