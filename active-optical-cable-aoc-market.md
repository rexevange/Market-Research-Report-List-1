<p><p>Active Optical Cable (AOC) is a type of optical fiber cable that uses electrical-to-optical conversion circuits to transmit data over long distances. It incorporates active components, such as lasers and photodetectors, within the cable itself, making it a complete data transmission solution. AOCs are widely used in applications that require high-speed data transfer with low latency, such as data centers, telecommunication networks, and high-performance computing.</p><p>The current outlook of the AOC market is positive and shows significant growth potential. The increasing demand for high-speed data transmission, driven by the proliferation of data-intensive applications and the rising adoption of cloud computing and IoT technologies, is one of the key factors fueling the market growth. AOCs offer several advantages over traditional copper cables, including longer transmission distances, higher bandwidth capacity, and immunity to electromagnetic interference, making them a preferred choice for high-speed data transmission applications.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing adoption of 5G technology and the need for faster and more reliable communication networks are expected to drive the demand for AOCs in the future. The development of advanced data center infrastructure, the expansion of fiber optic networks, and the increasing use of AOCs in emerging industries such as healthcare and aerospace are also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>According to the provided information, the AOC market is projected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 9.5% during the forecasted period.
