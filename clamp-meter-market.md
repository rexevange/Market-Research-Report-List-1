Hall effect clamp meters, on the other hand, can measure both AC and DC current and use the Hall effect sensor. Lastly, flexible clamp meters are designed for measuring AC current in tight spaces or around irregularly shaped conductors, offering convenient flexibility. Each type caters to specific needs, making them valuable tools in the electrical industry. These descriptions aim to provide a basic understanding of the different clamp meter types in the market.</p></p>
