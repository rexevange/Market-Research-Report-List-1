<p><p>Smart Battery Monitoring System (BMS) is a technology used to monitor the performance and health of batteries. There are two types of BMS markets - wired and wireless. Wired BMS uses physical connections like cables to transmit data between the batteries and the monitoring system. This type of BMS requires reliable connections and is commonly used in applications with fixed battery installations. On the other hand, wireless BMS uses wireless communication technologies to transmit battery data, eliminating the need for physical connections. This type of BMS is preferred in applications where mobility and flexibility are required, such as electric vehicles.</p></p>
