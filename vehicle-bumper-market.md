<p><p>The vehicle bumper market is divided into two main types: plastic bumper market and metal bumper market. They are widely used in modern vehicles due to their lower cost, easier manufacturing process, and ability to absorb impact. On the other hand, metal bumpers, made from steel or aluminum, provide greater strength and rigidity, offering better protection in high-impact collisions.
