<p><p>The portable pyrometer market includes two main types of technologies: optical and infrared. Optical pyrometers use visible light to measure temperature and require a direct line of sight to the object being measured. They are commonly used in industries like metalworking and glass manufacturing. In contrast, infrared pyrometers use infrared radiation to measure temperature and can do so from a distance without direct contact. They are widely employed in applications involving high temperatures, such as in foundries and power plants. Both types offer portable solutions for temperature measurement in various industries.</p></p>
