<p><p>The edible lecithin market comprises different types, including granules and capsules. Granules are small, easily dissolvable particles that can be added to various food and beverage products for their emulsifying properties. They help in stabilizing oil-water mixtures, preventing separation. Capsules, on the other hand, are a convenient way of consuming lecithin as a dietary supplement. They are typically filled with powdered lecithin and can be easily swallowed. Both granules and capsules cater to the growing demand for lecithin as a natural emulsifier and a source of essential phospholipids in the food and dietary supplement industries.</p></p>
