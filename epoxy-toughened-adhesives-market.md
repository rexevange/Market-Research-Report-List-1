<p><strong>The Epoxy Toughened Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubber Elastomer</li><li>Thermoplastic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy toughened adhesives are categorized into two main types: rubber elastomer and thermoplastic resin. Rubber elastomer adhesives are formulated by blending rubber particles with the epoxy resin, promoting flexibility and impact resistance in the adhesive. These adhesives are commonly used in applications that require strong bonding along with the ability to absorb shocks and vibrations. On the other hand, thermoplastic resin adhesives are made by incorporating thermoplastic polymers into the epoxy resin, enhancing the adhesive's heat resistance and durability. These adhesives are typically employed in high-temperature applications that demand strong bonding under extreme conditions.</p></p>
