There are three market types for BLIS: radar sensor, ultrasonic sensor, and other markets. The radar sensor uses radio waves to detect nearby objects, providing accurate and reliable blind spot information. Ultrasonic sensors, on the other hand, use sound waves to measure distances and can be used in BLIS systems. Lastly, the "other market" refers to alternative technologies or methods used in BLIS that are not specified as radar or ultrasonic sensors.</p></p>
