<p><p>Livestock electronic tags have two main types: plastic and metal. Plastic tags are made from durable materials such as polyurethane or polypropylene, which are lightweight and resistant to harsh environmental conditions. They can be easily attached to the animal's ear using a tagger. Metal tags, on the other hand, are typically made from stainless steel or aluminum. They are more durable and long-lasting compared to plastic tags, suitable for animals that may engage in rough activities. Both plastic and metal tags contain electronic components that allow for unique identification and tracking of livestock.</p></p>
