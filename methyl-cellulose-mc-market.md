<p><p>Methyl Cellulose (MC) is a chemical compound derived from cellulose, which is a naturally occurring polymer found in plant cell walls. The increasing demand for MC in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals and construction, drives the market growth. The growing pharmaceutical industry, particularly in developing regions, is expected to fuel the demand for MC.</p><p>In the construction industry, MC is extensively used as a cement additive to improve workability, water retention, and adhesion of mortar and concrete.
