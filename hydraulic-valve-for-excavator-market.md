<p><p>The hydraulic valve market for excavators can be classified into three types: pressure control, low control, and directional control. Pressure control valves regulate the hydraulic pressure within the system to prevent overloading or damage. Low control valves maintain a specified low pressure to achieve smooth and precise operation. Directional control valves determine the direction of hydraulic fluid flow, allowing the operator to control the movement of the excavator's various hydraulic components such as the boom, arm, and bucket. These valves play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of excavators.</p></p>
