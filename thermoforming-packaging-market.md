<p><strong>The Thermoforming Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blister Packs</li><li>Clamshells</li><li>Vacuum & Skin Packs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoforming packaging market includes various types of packaging options such as blister packs, clamshells, vacuum packs, and skin packs. Blister packs consist of a plastic cavity that holds the product and a backing material. Clamshells are hinged containers that enclose the product entirely. Vacuum packs involve removing the air from the package to maintain product freshness. Skin packs tightly adhere a thin plastic sheet to the product itself. These packaging options provide different levels of protection, visibility, and convenience, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
