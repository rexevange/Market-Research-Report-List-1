<p><p>Breast-shaped tissue expanders are medical devices used in breast reconstruction surgeries. There are two main types available in the market. Dual-port tissue expanders have two separate chambers for filling with saline solution, allowing for independent adjustment of both the upper and lower portions of the expander. On the other hand, one-port tissue expanders have a single chamber, with the saline solution being distributed throughout the entire device. These different types cater to varying patient needs and surgeon preferences, ensuring personalized and effective breast reconstruction outcomes.</p></p>
