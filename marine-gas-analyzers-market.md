<p><p>Marine gas analyzers are devices used to analyze and monitor the composition of gases in marine environments. They are mainly available in two types: portable and fixed. Portable marine gas analyzers are lightweight and compact, allowing for ease of mobility and instant on-site gas analysis. They are commonly used during inspections or on ships and boats operating in variable locations. Conversely, fixed marine gas analyzers are permanently installed at specific monitoring points and provide continuous monitoring of gas levels, ensuring safety and compliance with regulations in stationary marine settings.</p></p>
