<p><p>Dermatology diagnostic equipment encompasses various types of diagnostic tools used in the field of dermatology. Tissue diagnostics involve the examination and analysis of skin tissue samples to identify diseases or conditions. Molecular diagnostics employ techniques to detect and analyze DNA or RNA molecules in skin samples, aiding in the diagnosis of genetic disorders or infections. Hematology diagnostics focus on examining blood samples to identify blood disorders or systemic conditions that may affect the skin. These equipment types play crucial roles in accurately diagnosing and treating dermatological conditions.</p></p>
