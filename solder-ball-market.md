<p><p>The solder ball market consists of two main types: lead solder ball and lead-free solder ball. Lead solder ball is made with a mixture of metals that includes lead, while lead-free solder ball is made without lead, using alternative materials like tin, silver, and copper. Both types are used in electronic manufacturing, providing electrical connections and ensuring reliable bonding between electronic components. Lead solder ball has been widely used historically but is being phased out due to environmental concerns, with the lead-free solder ball becoming the preferred choice in many industries.</p></p>
