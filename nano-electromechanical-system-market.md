NEMS have various applications in industries such as electronics, healthcare, aerospace, and automotive.</p><p>The future outlook of the NEMS market appears promising. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the demand for point-of-care diagnostics, and the need for personalized medicine are expected to drive the adoption of NEMS in healthcare.</p><p>The electronics industry is also a key driver of the NEMS market. The miniaturization trend in electronics, coupled with the demand for faster and smaller devices, creates opportunities for NEMS-based sensors, switches, and actuators.
