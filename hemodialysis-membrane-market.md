<p><p>Hemodialysis membrane market is categorized into two types: cellulose base membrane and synthetic polymer membrane. Cellulose base membrane is made from natural cellulose and offers good biocompatibility but has limited permeability. On the other hand, synthetic polymer membrane is made from artificial materials and provides enhanced permeability, allowing for efficient removal of waste products. Both types of membranes are used in hemodialysis, a process that filters and purifies blood in patients with kidney dysfunction.</p></p>
