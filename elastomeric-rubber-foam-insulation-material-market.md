<p><p>The elastomeric rubber foam insulation material market can be classified into various types based on the type of rubber used. These types include NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based, EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR based insulation materials exhibit high resistance to oils and chemicals, making them suitable for industrial applications. EPDM based materials offer excellent weather and UV resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Similarly, chloroprene based insulation materials provide good resistance to flames and chemicals. Other types include silicone rubber foam insulation materials, which offer high temperature resistance and low toxicity.</p></p>
