<p><p>Software-Defined Perimeter (SDP) is a security approach that creates a dynamically constructed, secure network connection between the user and the resource they are trying to access. It establishes a secure perimeter around individual resources rather than the entire network, providing a more granular and targeted security solution. SDP focuses on user identity and device security, allowing organizations to implement zero-trust security principles and protect against threats such as unauthorized access, network attacks, and data breaches.</p><p>The global SDP market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend in the forecast period. Factors driving the growth of the market include the increasing adoption of cloud-based services, the rise in cyber-attacks, and the need for stringent data protection regulations.
