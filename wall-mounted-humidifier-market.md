<p><p>Wall-mounted humidifiers are devices used to increase the humidity level in a room or space by releasing moisture into the air. Vapor type humidifiers work by heating water to produce steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity. On the other hand, water spray humidifiers use a fan or ultrasonic technology to disperse water droplets into the air, providing a fine mist that increases moisture. Both types of humidifiers are designed to be mounted on walls for convenient and efficient use.</p></p>
