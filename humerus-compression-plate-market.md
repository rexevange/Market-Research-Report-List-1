<p><p>The humerus compression plate market comprises two types of plates: distal plate and proximal plate. Distal plates are specifically designed to be fixed at the distal end of the humerus bone, while proximal plates are designed for fixation at the proximal end. Both plates are commonly used in orthopedic surgeries to stabilize and align humerus fractures. Distal plates offer stability near the elbow joint, while proximal plates provide stability near the shoulder joint. These plates play a crucial role in the effective treatment of humerus bone fractures.</p></p>
