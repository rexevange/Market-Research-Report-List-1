<p><p>Household air humidifiers come in two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary humidifiers are designed to be placed in a fixed location within a home, usually connected to a water source and electrical outlet. They have higher water capacity and are suitable for larger rooms or whole house humidification. Portable humidifiers, on the other hand, are smaller and easy to move around. They usually have a smaller water tank and are ideal for smaller spaces or for people on the go. Both types serve the purpose of adding moisture to the air in order to improve indoor humidity levels.</p></p>
