<p><strong>The Elastomer Test System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tension Testing</li><li>Compression Testing</li><li>Adhesion Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Elastomer Test System market is divided into three types: tension testing, compression testing, and adhesion testing. Tension testing involves applying a pulling force to a sample of elastomer material to measure its strength and stretchability. Compression testing evaluates the ability of elastomers to withstand compression forces without losing their shape or properties. Adhesion testing determines the bonding strength between elastomers and other materials, assessing the quality of the adhesive used. These tests help manufacturers ensure the reliability and performance of elastomer products in various applications.</p></p>
