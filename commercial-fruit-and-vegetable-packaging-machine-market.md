<p><p>Commercial fruit and vegetable packaging machines are available in two main types: fully automatic packaging machines and semi-automatic packaging machines. Fully automatic packaging machines are capable of performing all packaging tasks without human intervention. They can sort, weigh, fill, seal, and label the products automatically. On the other hand, semi-automatic packaging machines require some amount of human involvement. While they can perform certain tasks automatically, they still rely on manual input for specific functions like filling or sealing. Both types cater to the needs of the commercial fruit and vegetable packaging industry.</p></p>
