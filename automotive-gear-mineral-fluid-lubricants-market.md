<p><p>The automotive gear mineral fluid lubricants market is categorized into three types: paraffinic, naphtenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are known for their excellent oxidation resistance and are commonly used in high-temperature applications. Naphtenic lubricants are preferred for their good solvency and low pour points, making them suitable for cold climates. Aromatic lubricants offer high viscosity index and good foam resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Each type has specific characteristics that cater to different automotive gear lubrication needs.</p></p>
