<p><p>The polymer additives market includes several types of additives that are used to enhance the performance and properties of polymers. Light stabilizers help prevent degradation of polymers caused by UV radiation, while processing stabilizers improve the processability of polymers during manufacturing. Antioxidants protect polymers from oxidation, sulfur-containing co-stabilizers work together with primary stabilizers to provide additional stability, and metal deactivators reduce the catalytic effect of trace metals. Antistatic agents are used to reduce or eliminate static electricity in polymers.</p></p>
