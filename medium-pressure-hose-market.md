<p><p>The medium pressure hose market is divided into two types: fabric reinforced and steel wire reinforced. Fabric reinforced hoses are constructed using multiple layers of fabric materials, such as polyester or nylon, which provide strength and flexibility. These hoses are suitable for applications where moderate pressure is involved, such as hydraulic systems. On the other hand, steel wire reinforced hoses are reinforced with steel wire braids, which enhance their durability and withstand higher pressure levels. These hoses are commonly used in heavy-duty industrial applications that require greater strength and resistance to abrasion.</p></p>
