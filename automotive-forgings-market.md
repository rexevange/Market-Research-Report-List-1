<p><strong>The Automotive Forgings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Forgings refer to the manufacturing process of shaping metal parts for vehicles through the use of pressure. Closed Die Forgings involve compressing heated metal between custom-made dies. This method is suitable for complex and precise components. Open Die Forgings involve shaping metal by hammering or pressing it into a specific shape without fully enclosing it in dies. Rolled Rings Forgings, on the other hand, involve shaping metal rings by rolling a heated cylindrical blank between two dies. These types of forgings are commonly used in the automotive industry to produce durable and high-quality parts.</p></p>
