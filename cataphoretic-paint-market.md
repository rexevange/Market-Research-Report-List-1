<p><p>Cataphoretic paint, also known as electrophoretic paint, is a type of coating used for corrosion protection and aesthetic enhancement in various industries. The market for cataphoretic paint is divided into two types: anodic electrophoretic paint and cathodic electrophoretic paint. </p><p>Anodic electrophoretic paint involves the deposition of a protective layer on the metal surface, creating a barrier against corrosion. Cathodic electrophoretic paint, on the other hand, uses the metal substrate as the negatively charged cathode, attracting positively charged paint particles. Both types offer efficient and durable protection against corrosion and are widely used in automotive, appliances, and other metal-based industries.</p></p>
