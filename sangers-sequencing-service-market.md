<p><p>Sanger sequencing service is offered in different market types including shotgun sequencing, targeted gene sequencing, and others. Shotgun sequencing involves breaking DNA into random fragments, sequencing them, and then assembling the final sequence. Targeted gene sequencing focuses on specific genes or regions of interest. Lastly, "others" encompass various specialized sequencing services like whole-genome sequencing, exome sequencing, or amplicon sequencing. These market types cater to different research needs by providing customized sequencing approaches to study specific genomic regions or the entire genome for various applications.</p></p>
