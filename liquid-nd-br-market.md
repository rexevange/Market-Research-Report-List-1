<p><p>The Liquid Nd-BR market comprises three types: Soluble Butadiene Rubber, Latex Polybutadiene Rubber, and Bulk Polymerized Sodium Butadiene Rubber. Soluble Butadiene Rubber is produced by dissolving the polymer in a solvent, making it suitable for applications like adhesives and coatings. Latex Polybutadiene Rubber is a milky liquid that finds use in manufacturing flexible products such as gloves and condoms. Bulk Polymerized Sodium Butadiene Rubber, on the other hand, involves the polymerization of butadiene monomers in a bulk phase, enabling its use in various industrial applications.</p></p>
