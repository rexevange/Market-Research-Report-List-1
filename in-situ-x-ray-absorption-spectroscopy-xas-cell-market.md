<p><p>In-situ X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (XAS) Cell market comprises two major types: Transmission (TM-XAS) and Fluorescent (FL-XAS). TM-XAS cells involve the transmission of X-rays through the sample, which allow for the characterization of the sample's atomic structure and chemical bonding. FL-XAS cells, on the other hand, involve the detection of fluorescence emitted from the sample upon X-ray excitation. FL-XAS is a useful technique for studying electronic structure and coordination environments of elements in complex systems. Both types of XAS cells find applications in a range of scientific and industrial fields.</p></p>
