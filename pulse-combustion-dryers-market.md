<p><p>Pulse combustion dryers are a type of drying equipment used in various industries. There are three main types of pulse combustion dryers: spray dryers, rotary dryers, and pneumatic dryers. Spray dryers use hot air to evaporate moisture from a liquid, converting it into a solid powder. Rotary dryers contain a rotating drum where materials are dried through direct contact with hot gases. Pneumatic dryers use a stream of high-velocity hot gas to rapidly dry materials suspended in the air. These dryers are widely used for efficient and rapid drying processes in different industries.</p></p>
