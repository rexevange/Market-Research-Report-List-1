<p><p>The Voice Coil Motor (VCM) industry consists of two main market types: AF VCM and OIS VCM. AF VCM (Auto Focus VCM) is utilized in camera modules of smartphones and digital cameras to adjust the focus of the lens automatically. This technology enables users to capture clear and focused images without manual intervention. On the other hand, OIS VCM (Optical Image Stabilization VCM) is employed to reduce image blur caused by camera movement. It compensates for handshakes and vibrations, resulting in steady and sharp photos and videos. Both AF VCM and OIS VCM play crucial roles in enhancing the photography experience.</p></p>
