<p><p>The curved display devices market encompasses three main types: LCD, OLED, and others. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technolgy is commonly used in curved TVs and computer monitors. It consists of a backlight source and liquid crystal pixels to create the image. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) technology is popular in curved smartphones and smartwatches. It operates by placing organic compounds between two electrodes to emit light when electric current passes through. The "others" category includes technologies like Quantum Dot and MicroLED, which offer different ways to achieve curved displays with enhanced color accuracy and contrast.</p></p>
