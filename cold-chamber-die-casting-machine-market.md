<p><p>The cold chamber die casting machine market consists of two types: manual and semi-automatic machines. In manual machines, the die casting process is carried out by human operators manually operating the machine. These machines require constant human intervention for tasks like pouring the molten metal, closing the die, and ejecting the casting. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines are equipped with some automation features. They can perform certain operations automatically, such as closing and opening the die, while still requiring human operators for other tasks.</p></p>
