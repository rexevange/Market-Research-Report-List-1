<p><p>PMMA resin flooring has two types: optical purpose and general purpose. Optical purpose PMMA resin flooring is designed to enhance visual appeal by utilizing its transparent and glossy properties. It is commonly used in showrooms, exhibition halls, and retail spaces where a visually striking flooring is desired. On the other hand, general purpose PMMA resin flooring offers durability, chemical resistance, and low maintenance, making it suitable for industrial and commercial applications such as warehouses, factories, and hospitals. These two types cater to different needs, providing either enhanced aesthetics or practical functionality.</p></p>
