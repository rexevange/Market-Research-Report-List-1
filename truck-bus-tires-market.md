<p><p>The truck and bus tires market can be classified into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of fabric cords that run at different angles across the tire. They offer better load-carrying capacity and are less expensive. On the other hand, radial tires have fabric cords that run perpendicular to the tread, which enhances stability and fuel efficiency. They are more durable and provide a smoother ride. These two tire types cater to different customer needs and preferences in the truck and bus industry.</p></p>
