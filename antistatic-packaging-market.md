<p><p>The antistatic packaging market comprises several types of products, namely anti-static bags, anti-static sponges, anti-static grids, and others. Anti-static bags are made of specialized materials that prevent the build-up of static electricity, protecting electronic components during transportation or storage. Anti-static sponges are used for cleaning purposes and effectively dissipate static charges. Anti-static grids, on the other hand, are conductive mats or sheets that offer protection by grounding static discharges. The "others" category includes additional products such as anti-static tapes, foam, trays, and containers that serve similar purposes in safeguarding sensitive electronic devices.</p></p>
