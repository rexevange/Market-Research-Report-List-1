<p><p>The urethane coating additive market includes two main types of polyurethane coatings: one-component polyurethane coatings and two-component polyurethane coatings. One-component polyurethane coatings consist of a single component that does not require any additional mixing before application. They are convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, two-component polyurethane coatings require the mixing of two separate components before application. They offer a higher level of performance and durability than one-component coatings, but require more effort in terms of preparation and application.</p></p>
