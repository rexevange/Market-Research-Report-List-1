<p><p>Automotive brake wear indicators refer to mechanisms that provide alerts about the condition of brake pads in vehicles. Electrical indicators are a type of brake wear indicator that uses an electrical circuit to monitor brake pad wear and trigger a warning light on the dashboard when replacement is needed. Audible indicators, on the other hand, produce a screeching or squealing noise when the brake pads are worn out. Both types serve as safety features to ensure timely replacement and maintenance of brake pads, preventing potential accidents and damage to braking systems.</p></p>
