<p><p>Construction stone market types refer to the various materials used in construction projects. Limestone, commonly used for foundations and walls, is a durable sedimentary rock. Marble, prized for its elegance, is a metamorphic rock often found in decorative elements like sculptures and tiles. Other materials, such as sandstone and slate, are also used in construction due to their unique qualities like weather resistance and versatility. These market types cater to specific construction needs and preferences.</p></p>
