<p><p>The hydrogen gas generator market includes two types of technologies: traditional alkaline electrolyzer and PEM electrolyzer. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers operate by using an alkaline solution as an electrolyte to generate hydrogen gas through the process of water electrolysis. On the other hand, PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane to separate the hydrogen and oxygen gases, resulting in a more efficient and compact hydrogen generation process. Both technologies play a crucial role in producing hydrogen gas for various industrial applications and the growing demand for clean and sustainable energy sources.</p></p>
