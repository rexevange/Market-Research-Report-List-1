<p><p>In the market for YVO4 crystals, there are two main types: pure YVO4 crystals and Nd YVO4 crystals. Pure YVO4 crystals refer to the ones that are made solely of yttrium orthovanadate (YVO4). These crystals are highly sought after for their excellent optical properties, including high refractive index and low absorption. On the other hand, Nd YVO4 crystals are doped with neodymium (Nd), which gives them additional advantages for laser applications. Nd YVO4 crystals have gain medium properties, making them ideal for use in solid-state lasers for various industrial and scientific purposes.</p></p>
