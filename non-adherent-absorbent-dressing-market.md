<p><p>The non-adherent absorbent dressing market includes two main types: dry dressing and wet dressing. Dry dressing is applied to wounds that have no or minimal drainage. It absorbs any moisture or exudate from the wound and provides a protective layer. Wet dressing, on the other hand, is used for wounds with heavy drainage. It contains a gel-like substance that keeps the wound moist and helps with healing. Both types of dressings prevent sticking to the wound, allowing easy and painless removal during dressing changes.</p></p>
