<p><p>Brown Fused Alumina (BFA) is a popular abrasive material used in various industries. The market can be categorized into two types based on the type of furnace used during the production process. The Dumping Furnace Type involves pouring molten aluminum oxide into a bed of carbon materials, allowing it to solidify and be crushed into abrasive grains. The Fixed Furnace Type, on the other hand, uses an electric arc furnace to melt and refine the raw materials into BFA. These different types of furnaces result in variations in BFA quality and cost.</p></p>
