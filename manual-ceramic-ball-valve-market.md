The valve body is made of ceramic material, which offers excellent resistance to corrosion and wear, making it suitable for use in harsh operating conditions.</p><p>The global manual ceramic ball valve market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The market for manual ceramic ball valves can be categorized into three types based on temperature requirements. </p><p>1. Low Temperature Valve: These valves are designed to operate in extreme cold conditions, such as cryogenic applications. They are built with special materials and insulation to prevent freezing and maintain smooth operation.</p><p>2. Normal Temperature Valve: This type of valve is suitable for regular temperature ranges commonly found in industrial processes. They offer reliable performance and durability for various applications and are generally more affordable than high-temperature valves.</p><p>3. High Temperature Valve: These valves are specifically engineered to withstand extremely high temperatures, such as those encountered in refining, chemical, or power generation industries. They are constructed with materials that can withstand thermal expansion and maintain tight sealing under extreme heat conditions.</p></p>
