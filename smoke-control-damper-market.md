<p><p>The smoke control damper market is categorized into various types. Mechanical dampers are designed to regulate airflow in HVAC systems and prevent the spread of smoke during a fire. Intumescent dampers contain a material that expands when exposed to heat, forming a seal and preventing smoke from passing through. Air transfer fire dampers are used in air ducts to limit the spread of smoke and fire. Other types of dampers include electric dampers and pneumatic dampers, which function similarly to mechanical dampers but are operated by different mechanisms.</p></p>
