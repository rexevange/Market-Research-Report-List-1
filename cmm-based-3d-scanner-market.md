These scanners are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, architecture, healthcare, and manufacturing for quality control, inspection, reverse engineering, and dimensional analysis purposes.</p><p>The CMM Based 3D Scanner Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
