Mobile applications allow surgeons and healthcare professionals to access patient data and surgical information anytime, anywhere.</p><p>Overall, the surgery management software market is projected to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by advancements in technology, increasing surgical procedures, and the need for efficient healthcare management systems.</p></p>
