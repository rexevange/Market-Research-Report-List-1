<p><p>The Crosslinked Polyethylene (PEX) Tube market offers three types of PEX tubes - PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. These types differ in terms of manufacturing processes and properties. PE-Xa is produced using the Peroxide method, which results in excellent temperature resistance and shape memory. PE-Xb is created through the Silane method, offering good temperature resistance but limited shape memory. PE-Xc is manufactured using the Electron Beam method, providing moderate temperature resistance and shape memory. Each type caters to various applications, allowing customers to choose based on their specific needs.</p></p>
