<p><p>Adult orthotic insoles are specialized shoe inserts used to provide support and comfort for individuals with various foot conditions. There are two main types of adult orthotic insoles: half palm and full palm. Half palm insoles provide arch support from the midfoot to the heel, while full palm insoles extend support to the entire foot, including the forefoot. These insoles are designed to alleviate foot pain, improve stability, and correct foot alignment. They are commonly used by adults suffering from conditions such as plantar fasciitis, flat feet, and arthritis.</p></p>
