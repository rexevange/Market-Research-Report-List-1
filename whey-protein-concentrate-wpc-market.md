<p><p>Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC) is a type of protein powder derived from cow's milk during the cheese production process. The market is primarily driven by the increasing consumer awareness about the health benefits of protein supplementation, growing demand for functional foods and beverages, and rising interest in fitness and sports activities worldwide. Additionally, the rise in disposable income, changing lifestyles, and the emergence of e-commerce platforms have also contributed to the market growth.</p><p>Several trends are shaping the Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC) market. Secondly, manufacturers are focusing on product innovation and launching new flavors, ingredients, and formats to cater to the diverse consumer preferences and dietary requirements.
