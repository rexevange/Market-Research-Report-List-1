<p><strong>The Vehicle Smart Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Smart Key Market consists of two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES utilizes proximity sensors to automatically unlock/lock the vehicle's doors when the smart key is in close range. It enables keyless access without pressing any buttons. On the other hand, RKES allows users to lock/unlock the vehicle using a remote control. It requires pressing a button on the remote to send signals to the vehicle's locking system, providing wireless control over access to the vehicle.</p></p>
