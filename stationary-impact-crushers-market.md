<p><strong>The Stationary Impact Crushers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Impact</li><li>Vertical Shaft Impact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary impact crushers are classified into two main types: horizontal shaft impact crushers and vertical shaft impact crushers. </p><p>Horizontal shaft impact crushers (HSI) have a horizontal shaft that rotates at high speed, impacting the material fed into the crusher. These crushers are known for producing excellent cubic-shaped end products and are commonly used in mining and construction industries.</p><p>Vertical shaft impact crushers (VSI) use a different approach, as they utilize a vertical shaft that rotates at high speed to create a crushing motion. VSI crushers are known for producing high-quality and consistent-shaped aggregates, making them suitable for various applications such as sand production and recycling.</p></p>
