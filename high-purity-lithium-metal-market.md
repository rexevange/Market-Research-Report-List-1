<p><p>The high-purity lithium metal market is divided into two main types: Salt Lake Brine and Lithium Ore. In the Salt Lake Brine market, lithium is extracted from underground reservoirs known as brine pools found in salt lakes. This extraction process involves the evaporation of water from the brine, leaving behind lithium-rich deposits. On the other hand, the Lithium Ore market involves mining lithium-bearing minerals, such as spodumene or petalite, from ore deposits. These minerals are then processed and refined to obtain high-purity lithium metal.</p></p>
