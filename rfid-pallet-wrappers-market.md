<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Pallet Wrappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li><li>High Frequency and Near Field Communication</li><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID pallet wrapper market can be categorized into different types based on their frequency bands. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) pallet wrappers operate within the 860-960 MHz range, offering long-range communication and high read rate. High-Frequency (HF) and Near Field Communication (NFC) pallet wrappers operate at 13.56 MHz and are commonly used for shorter-range applications. Low-Frequency (LF) pallet wrappers operate at 125-134 kHz and are known for their ability to penetrate liquids and metals. Dual-frequency pallet wrappers support both UHF and HF/NFC frequencies, providing flexibility in different applications.</p></p>
