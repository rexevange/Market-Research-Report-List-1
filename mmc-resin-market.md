<p><p>The MMC resin market includes two main types: ion exchange - hydrophobic type and hydroxyapatite type. Ion exchange - hydrophobic type refers to resins that have a high affinity for ions and possess hydrophobic properties, meaning they repel water. These resins are commonly used in applications such as water treatment, purification of pharmaceuticals, and removal of heavy metal ions. On the other hand, hydroxyapatite type resins are biomaterials that have excellent biocompatibility and are utilized in various medical fields, including bone tissue engineering and drug delivery systems.</p></p>
