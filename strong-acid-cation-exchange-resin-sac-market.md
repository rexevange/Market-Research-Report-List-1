<p><p>Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin (SAC) is a type of resin used in water treatment processes for removing cations from water. There are two main types of SAC resins: Gel Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin and Macroporous Strong Acid Cation Exchange Resin. </p><p>Gel SAC resin has a rigid and compact structure, making it highly efficient for removing moderate to high concentrations of cations. </p><p>Macroporous SAC resin, on the other hand, has a porous structure that allows for greater capacity and faster ion exchange, making it suitable for high flow rate applications or when dealing with heavy metal ions.</p></p>
