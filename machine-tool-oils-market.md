<p><p>Machine tool oils, also known as metalworking fluids, are essential for lubricating and cooling various machine tools during the manufacturing process. There are different types of machine tool oils, including hydraulic oil, mineral oil, and sideway oil. Hydraulic oil is utilized for hydraulic systems in machine tools, ensuring smooth operation and preventing wear and tear. Mineral oil, often used in cutting and grinding operations, provides lubrication and cooling to the tooling and workpiece. Sideway oil is specifically designed to lubricate the slideways of machine tools, reducing friction and facilitating precise movement. These oils play vital roles in maximizing the efficiency and lifespan of machine tools.</p></p>
