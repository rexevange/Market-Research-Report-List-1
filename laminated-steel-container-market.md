<p><p>The laminated steel container market consists of two main types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans are made from a single piece of laminated steel that is molded into shape and then sealed with a bottom end to create a container. These cans are commonly used for beverages, such as carbonated drinks, due to their ability to withstand the pressure.</p><p>3-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from three separate components: a top end, a body, and a bottom end. These cans offer greater flexibility in terms of size and shape, making them suitable for various products like food items, aerosols, and speciality chemicals.</p></p>
