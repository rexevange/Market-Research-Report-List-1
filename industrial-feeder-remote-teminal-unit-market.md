<p><p>The Industrial Feeder Remote Terminal Unit (FRTU) market consists of two main types: wireless and wired FRTUs. Wireless FRTUs use wireless communication technologies, such as Wi-Fi or cellular networks, to transmit data between the industrial feeder and the central control system. This enables remote monitoring and control without the need for physical cables. On the other hand, wired FRTUs rely on physical wired connections, such as Ethernet or serial cables, to establish communication. This ensures a stable and secure transmission of data between the industrial feeder and the central control system.</p></p>
