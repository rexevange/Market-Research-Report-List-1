<p><p>Transformer insulation paper is used in electrical transformers to provide insulation and enhance their performance. There are various types available in the market, including Nomex, DMD (Dacron Mylar Dacron), NMN (Nomex Mylar Nomex), and other varieties. Nomex paper is made of aramid fibers and offers high-temperature resistance. DMD is composed of polyester film and non-woven polyester and provides excellent dielectric properties. NMN consists of Nomex paper sandwiched between two layers of polyester film, offering superior thermal resistance and mechanical strength. Other types may include materials like crepe paper, kraft paper, and fish paper, each with specific features suitable for different transformer applications.</p></p>
