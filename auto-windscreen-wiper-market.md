<p><p>Auto windscreen wiper market is categorized into two types: boneless wipers and bone wipers. Boneless wipers have a streamlined design without any external metal structure, resulting in reduced wind noise and better performance at high speeds. They are also more aesthetically pleasing. On the other hand, bone wipers have a conventional design with external metal frames. While they may produce more wind noise, they are generally more affordable and widely available. Both types serve the purpose of keeping the windscreen clean during rainy conditions for improved visibility while driving.</p></p>
