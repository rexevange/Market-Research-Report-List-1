<p><p>The oilfield drilling additives market includes various types of additives that are used in drilling operations to enhance performance and efficiency. Corrosion inhibitors prevent the corrosion of equipment. Biocides eliminate harmful microorganisms. Surface modifiers enhance the surface properties of drilling fluids. Defoamers remove foam and air bubbles from fluids. Lastly, "others" category includes various additives such as lubricants, surfactants, and pH control agents used in drilling operations.</p></p>
