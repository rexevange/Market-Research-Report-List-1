<p><p>Leukemia therapeutic market types include induction therapy, consolidation therapy, and maintenance therapy. Induction therapy aims to eradicate the majority of leukemia cells in the body, leading to remission. Consolidation therapy follows induction therapy and eliminates any remaining leukemia cells to prevent relapse. Maintenance therapy aims to prevent the growth of any residual leukemia cells over the long term. These different types of therapies are used sequentially in the treatment of leukemia to improve the chances of achieving a complete cure and prolonging patient survival.</p></p>

<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Leukemia Therapeutic market?</strong></p>
<p><p>Emerging trends in the global leukemia therapeutic market include the advent of targeted therapies, advancements in immunotherapy, and the use of combination therapies.
