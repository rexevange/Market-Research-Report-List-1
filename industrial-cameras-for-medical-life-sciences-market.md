<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial cameras for the medical & life sciences market are classified into two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a 2D image of a stationary object or scene, providing high-resolution images for detailed analysis. These cameras are commonly used for applications such as microscopy, pathology, and laboratory imaging. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images of moving objects or scenes line by line, producing high-quality images of continuous objects. This technology is widely used in applications like tissue inspection, quality control, and production line monitoring in the medical and life sciences industry.</p></p>
