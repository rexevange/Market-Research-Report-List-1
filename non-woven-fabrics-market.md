<p><p>Non-woven fabrics market can be classified into various types. Spunbond non-woven fabric is made by bonding fibers together with heat and pressure. Spunlace non-woven fabric is produced by entangling fibers using water jets. Needle punch non-woven fabric is created by mechanically interlocking fibers with barbed needles. Meltblown non-woven fabric is formed by melting and extruding polymers to create fine fibers. Wet laid non-woven fabric is manufactured by forming a fibrous suspension into a sheet. The market also includes other types of non-woven fabrics serving different applications.</p></p>
