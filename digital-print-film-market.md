<p><strong>The Digital Print Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital print film market consists of various types, including solvent-based film and water-based film. Solvent-based film is a type of film that uses solvents as the main component for the ink, which is then printed onto the film surface. This type of film offers high durability and excellent weather resistance. On the other hand, water-based film uses water as the main component for the ink. This type of film is more environmentally friendly and suitable for applications that require lower levels of durability.</p></p>
