<p><p>A flat retort pouch is a flexible packaging solution that is primarily used for the packaging of cooked food. Several factors are contributing to the growth of this market. Firstly, the increasing demand for convenience food and ready-to-eat meals is driving the demand for flat retort pouches as they provide a lightweight, portable, and easy-to-use packaging solution. Secondly, the rising awareness about food safety and hygiene is propelling the adoption of retort pouches as they offer better protection against contamination and spoilage. Additionally, the growing popularity of sustainable and eco-friendly packaging materials is spurring the demand for flat retort pouches made from recyclable or biodegradable materials.</p><p>Furthermore, the booming e-commerce industry is also playing a significant role in the growth of the flat retort pouch market. As more consumers are leaning towards online grocery shopping, the need for secure and durable packaging solutions, like the flat retort pouch, is increasing to ensure the safe delivery of food products.</p><p>Overall, the future outlook for the flat retort pouch market is promising, driven by the increasing demand for convenience food, rising food safety concerns, growing environmental consciousness, and the flourishing e-commerce sector.</p></p>
